Recipe for Chili

Will your chili look this good?

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef
  • tomato sauce
  • beans
  • onions
  • garlic
  • chili powder

Steps:

  1. Cook beans in water until soft
  2. brown beef with onions and garlic
  3. mix together with chili powder and tomato sauce
  4. simmer for 30 minutes and salt to taste
