Recipe for Eggplant Parmesan

Will your eggplant parmesan look this good?

Ingredients:

  • Eggplant
  • tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • egg
  • bread crumbs
  • flour
  • oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Slice and salt eggplant. Leave for several hours to drain
  2. dip eggplant in flour, then eggs, then bread crumbs. Fry until golden brown
  3. cover eggplant with tomato sauce and cheese. Bake at 400 degrees until cheese is sufficiently browned
